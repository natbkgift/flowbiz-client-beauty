# Pilot Discovery Questionnaire - FlowBiz Beauty

Date: 2026-05-27
Scope: sales discovery and pilot qualification

## Purpose

Use this questionnaire before a friendly pilot. The goal is to understand clinic workflow, lead leakage, repeat revenue opportunities, staff readiness, and AI safety concerns.

This questionnaire does not collect medical records and should not request unnecessary sensitive data.

## Lead Sources

- Where do leads come from today?
- Approximate leads per month?
- Which channel creates the most missed follow-up?
- Which channel has highest booking intent?
- Are leads assigned to specific staff?
- How do staff know a lead has not been contacted?
- Is lead source tracked consistently?

## Response Workflow

- What is the expected first response time?
- What is the real average first response time?
- What happens outside business hours?
- Who writes follow-up messages?
- Are there approved templates?
- How are hot leads identified?
- What causes leads to drop off most often?

## No-Show Process

- How often do consults or appointments become no-shows?
- Who follows up after a no-show?
- Is there a standard message?
- How quickly is follow-up sent?
- Are no-show outcomes tracked?
- What would count as a successful recovery?

## Review Request Process

- Does the clinic request reviews after aftercare or treatment?
- Who asks for reviews?
- Which channel is used?
- Is there a standard timing rule?
- Are review outcomes tracked?
- What language should be avoided?

## Repeat Customer Workflow

- How does the clinic track Botox repeat cycles?
- How does the clinic track filler repeat cycles?
- Who owns repeat reminders?
- Is the timing tracked in CRM/LINE/spreadsheet?
- How many customers are currently due or overdue?
- What message tone works best for repeat reminders?

## CRM And LINE Usage

- Which CRM, if any, is used today?
- Is LINE OA the main operational inbox?
- Does staff use tags, notes, or labels?
- Are templates shared across staff?
- Is there a reporting dashboard?
- What should FlowBiz not replace during pilot?

## AI Concerns

- What worries the team about AI?
- What messages should AI never draft?
- What medical or claim language should be avoided?
- Who should approve AI suggestions?
- Should high-risk wording require manager review?
- What would make staff trust AI suggestions more?

## Staff Readiness

- How many staff will use FlowBiz weekly?
- Which staff will approve suggestions?
- Who can reject/edit AI drafts?
- How much training time is available?
- Is daily queue review realistic?
- What workflow would staff adopt first?

## Success Criteria

Ask the clinic to rank:

- Faster first response
- Fewer uncontacted leads
- More consistent follow-up
- Better no-show recovery
- More review requests
- More repeat reminders
- Less manual staff work
- Better audit visibility

Define pilot success:

```text
Pilot is successful if <metric/workflow/adoption target>.
```
