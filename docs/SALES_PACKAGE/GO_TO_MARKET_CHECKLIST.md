## Messaging Guardrails

Do not claim:

- FlowBiz is a full EMR
- FlowBiz replaces doctor judgement
- FlowBiz is a full scheduling/inventory/payment system
- AI sends customer-facing messages without approval
- Real LINE send is active by default
- Real AI generation is active by default
- Business outcome is promised

Do say:

- FlowBiz is an AI Marketing and Revenue Automation Layer
- AI drafts, staff approves
- MVP is demo-ready with simulated/default integration controls
- Staging deployment requires live smoke before broader pilot usage
- Production requires additional consent, security, integration, and support readiness

## Pilot Qualification Checklist

- Clinic has meaningful lead flow
- Clinic has staff owner
- Clinic has decision maker in pilot
- Clinic has repeat-treatment opportunity
- Clinic accepts HITL rule
- Clinic accepts pilot metrics
- Clinic understands current integration limits
- Clinic does not require full EMR/scheduling/inventory/payment in MVP

## Sales Process Checklist

1. Discovery call completed
2. Pain mapped to MVP workflows
3. Demo completed
4. Limitations explained
5. Pilot scope selected
6. Pilot dates proposed
7. Pilot owner identified
8. Pricing range discussed
9. Next meeting scheduled
10. Follow-up summary sent

## Readiness Gate

Internal demo:

- Demo seed runs
- Demo script ready
- Sales package ready

Friendly pilot demo:

- Staging or local demo environment stable
- Limitations explained upfront
- No real-send policy accepted

Staging pilot:

- Live smoke test passes
- Env is staging-only
- No production credentials
- CI remains green

Production:

- Not a Phase 10 claim
- Requires separate readiness review
